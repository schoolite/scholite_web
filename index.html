We are on a mission to
empower Nigerian youth

We focus on training secondary school pupils with a year program preparing them for university life with a program determined to enhance mental ability, productivity, skill acquisition and employability

The process Fund a teen

1 .Enrollment

Parents, Guardians, Schools and partnering bodies enroll pupils through direct payment or part sponsorship

2. Train

Pupils are train using the designed self-paced curiculum for a peroid of one year. This includes Book reading and review, techincal skills acquisition, guest lecture series from professionals, fun activities and more

3. Follow up

After the program, pupils are remotely mentored by alumni and professionals with portal for linking them to available opportunities and clustering for solutions development
